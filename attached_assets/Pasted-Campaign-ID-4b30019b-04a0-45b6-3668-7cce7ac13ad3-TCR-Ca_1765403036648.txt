Message flow
Digital Opt-In: Customers provide their phone number on our booking page at https://sillydog-app.onrender.com/book. The form includes a clear SMS consent statement: “By providing your phone number, you agree to receive SMS messages from Silly Dog Pooper Scooper about service reminders, scheduling updates, on-the-way alerts, invoices, and customer support. Message & data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for help.” Keyword Opt-In: Customers may text START or YES to our business number to opt in. After texting START/YES, they receive a confirmation message explaining the program, message frequency, STOP/HELP instructions, and that message & data rates may apply. STOP Command: If a customer texts STOP or UNSUBSCRIBE, they are immediately unsubscribed and receive a confirmation message stating they will receive no further texts. HELP Command: If a customer texts HELP, they receive details about our notifications, message frequency, and how to reach customer support. Message Types: We send appointment reminders, on-the-way alerts, job completion updates, schedule changes, and invoice notifications. No marketing or promotional messages are sent.
